About

Our purpose is to stimulate an interest in the science of Astronomy. We will further educate young minds on the science and wonders of Astronomy by viewing the sun, moon, stars, and other parts of our solar system. We try to expand our own knowledge of the stars and our universe. Because you can never know enough about the ever expanding cosmos.
